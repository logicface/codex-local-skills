# Research Writing Length Control

Use this skill for controlled shortening or expansion of academic text.

## Core rules

- Preserve all core claims, variables, conditions, and experimental details.
- Keep the change local and moderate unless the user explicitly asks for major rewriting.
- Provide a brief modification log when the user would benefit from checking what changed.
- Do not add invented evidence during expansion.

## Shorten

- Remove redundancy, filler, and verbose syntax.
- Prefer syntactic compression over content deletion.
- Keep equations, parameter values, and constraints intact.

## Expand

- Add only reasonable connective logic, implicit premises, or clearer phrasing.
- Prefer precision and continuity, not decorative wording.
- Keep the paragraph academically compact even after expansion.
