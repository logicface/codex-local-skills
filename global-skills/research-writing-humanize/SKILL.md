# Research Writing Humanize

Use this skill to remove mechanical, inflated, or overly model-like academic phrasing.

## Core rules

- Prefer natural, precise, field-standard wording.
- Remove empty rhetorical emphasis, cliché transitions, and vocabulary chosen only for grandiosity.
- Do not rewrite aggressively if the text is already natural.
- Preserve technical terminology and exact claims.

## English mode

- Replace overused pseudo-sophisticated wording with plain scientific language.
- Reduce robotic transitions and excessive punctuation tricks.
- Keep LaTeX content clean and paragraph-based.

## Chinese mode

- Remove translationese, slogan-like phrasing, and decorative academic clichés.
- Prefer natural written Chinese over literal English-style structure.
- Keep output Word-friendly and free of Markdown formatting.
